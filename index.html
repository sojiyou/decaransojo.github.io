Welcome!

Hello, my name is Sojo Sales A. Decaran, and I am 21 years old. Originally from Loacan, Itogon, Benguet, I am currently residing in Brookside, Baguio City. I have always been passionate about activities that engage both my mind and body—whether it’s playing volleyball, coding, listening to music, watching anime, or diving into mobile games.

Currently, I am pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, specializing in Web Technologies, at the University of the Cordilleras.

I chose Information Technology because it offers a wide range of specializations—whether in web development, network security, or computer science—each presenting a future filled with opportunities. What draws me the most to IT is its focus on problem-solving, from debugging code to planning intuitive user interfaces. These challenges align with my personal learning style, where I thrive on solving complex problems and developing creative solutions.

Moreover, IT is a field that's continuously evolving, and its demand is increasing rapidly as businesses and industries shift towards digital platforms. I see this trend as a gateway to a promising career where I can contribute to meaningful technological advancements. While there are many reasons behind my choice of this path, I’m confident that IT will allow me to merge my passion for technology with my desire for continuous learning and growth.
